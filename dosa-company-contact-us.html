Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer catering services?
Yes! We offer catering for events of all sizes. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance to discuss your requirements and place an order.
Are your dosas gluten-free?
Traditional dosas are naturally gluten-free as they're made from rice and lentil batter. However, we recommend informing our staff about any allergies to ensure we take extra precautions during preparation.
Do you deliver?
Yes, we offer delivery within a 10km radius of our restaurant. You can place your order through our website or by calling us directly.
Do you have vegan options?
Absolutely! Many of our dosas and accompaniments are vegan-friendly or can be made vegan upon request. Just let our staff know about your dietary preferences.
